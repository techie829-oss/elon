Overview
Experion Sector 151 Noida

Experion Sector 151 on Noida Expressway is a newly launched luxury residential project by Experion Group, offering premium Experion 3 & 4 BHK apartments designed for modern, elegant, and spacious living. Spread across 5 acres along the Noida–Greater Noida Expressway, this Experion low-density project features 3 towers (G+35) with just 425 premium apartments, ensuring unmatched privacy and exclusivity. Each apartment comes with large layouts (2100–3200 sq. ft.), maximum ventilation, natural light, and experion premium interiors. Built by a Grade A contractor Shapoorji Pallonji, construction partner DPS projects, architect partner Belt Collins, the project showcases superior construction quality and architectural finesse. Experion’s project expected possession time is December 2030, giving homebuyers enough time to plan their investment and future move. The project offers Experion world-class amenities, including a 50,000 sq.ft.clubhouse, gym, swimming pool, landscaped gardens, sports zones, and 24x7 security, along with smart home features and eco-friendly design. Located in Experion Sector 151 Noida, it provides excellent connectivity to the Noida- Greater Noida Expressway, top schools, business hubs, hospitals, Sector 148 (Aqua Metro line) metro station, and the upcoming Jewar International Airport, this project stands out as a strong competitor to some of the top real estate companies in India in terms of lifestyle and value.
